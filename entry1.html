How I got started Cubing







I first started cubing when I was in around the fourth grade. I was extremely intrigued by the idea of solving such a difficult puzzle. During summer camp after the school year was over, I spent a lot of my free time trying to figure it out. Using the official instructions that came with the cube, I was able to almost always get to the last step. However, I would always scre up after I got to it. Then, one day an older kid walked up to me and said it was impossible. This only made me even more determined to succeed.
When I got home that day, I decided to look it up online. The first thing that came up was a tutorial by a youtuber known as Rob's World. With it, I proceeded to solve the Rubik's Cube for my first time, and most importantly, amazing the kid who said I couldn't do it.
